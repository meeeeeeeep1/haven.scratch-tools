Learn how to use the Custom Scratch Font Tool without having to download anything from the website.

Step 1: Click on the the Copy Svg Code Button

Step 2: Go to svgviewer.dev and paste the svg

Step 3: Click the download svg button

✨And Now Your Done! ✨
